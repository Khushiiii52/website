Popular Temples

Kedarnath Temple

Kedarnath is a sacred Hindu town in Uttarakhand, India, famous for the Kedarnath Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. Nestled in the Himalayas, it is a major pilgrimage site and part of the Char Dham Yatra.

Prem Mandir

Prem Mandir is a grand Hindu temple located in Vrindavan, Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to Radha Krishna and Sita Ram. Built by Jagadguru Kripaluji Maharaj, it is known for its stunning white marble architecture and beautifully illuminated evening light shows.

Vaishno Devi Temple

Vaishno Devi Temple in Jammu is a sacred Hindu shrine dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi. Devotees trek 12 km to reach the holy cave housing her sacred Pindis.

Monuments

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal in Agra is a white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of eternal love.

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is a 73-meter tall minaret in Delhi, built in 1193 by Qutb-ud-din Aibak to celebrate Muslim dominance in Delhi after the defeat of the last Hindu kingdom. It is made of red sandstone and marble, and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its intricate carvings and inscriptions.

Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple in Delhi is a Bahá'í House of Worship known for its flower-like shape and serene atmosphere. Made of white marble, it symbolizes unity and is open to people of all religions for meditation and prayer.

Featured Activities

River Rafting

River rafting is an adventurous outdoor activity where individuals navigate through rivers using inflatable rafts, often in rapid or turbulent waters. It combines teamwork, excitement, and the thrill of nature as participants work together to control the raft while encountering various challenges.

Paragliding

Paragliding is an aerial sport where individuals glide through the air using a lightweight, non-motorized parachute-like wing called a paraglider. It offers a thrilling experience of flying while enjoying panoramic views of landscapes, and it's often done from mountains or hills.

Trekking

Trekking is a long, challenging journey on foot, usually through natural terrains like mountains, forests, or hills. It combines physical endurance and adventure, offering stunning views and a close connection to nature.
